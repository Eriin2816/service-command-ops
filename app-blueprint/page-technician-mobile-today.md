# Page: Technician Mobile Today

## Path
/tech/today

## Purpose
Technician opens app on phone → sees today's jobs in order.

## Design Requirements
- Mobile-first, one-handed operation
- Large tap targets
- Job cards showing: address, service type, priority
- Tap card → open job detail
- Status at a glance

## Key Components
- Date header (today's date)
- Job card list (ordered by scheduled time)
- Each card: customer name, address, service type, status badge
- CTA: "Start Job" button on each card
