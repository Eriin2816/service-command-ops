# Product Boundaries — Non-Negotiable Rules

## Do NOT Build (GHL Already Does This)
- CRM or contact management system
- Conversations / messaging (SMS, email, chat)
- Lead capture forms or landing pages
- SMS or email automation workflows
- Sales pipeline or opportunity management (mirror only)
- Calendar or booking system
- Marketing automation
- Missed-call text-back

## When ServiceOps Starts
The app starts AFTER a lead becomes:
- Appointment Booked (in GHL calendar)
- Opportunity Won (in GHL pipeline)
- Needs Service (custom GHL tag or stage)

## Scope Check Rule
Before adding any feature, ask: "Does GHL already do this?"
If yes → do not build it. Integrate with GHL instead.
