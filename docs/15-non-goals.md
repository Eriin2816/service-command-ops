- A customer-facing portal
- A billing and invoicing system
- A payment processor
- A route optimizer
- A dispatch board with map view
- A native iOS or Android app (Phase 1 is mobile web)
- An SMS or email messaging platform
- A marketing automation tool
- An inventory management system
- An AI voice agent
- A white-label billing platform (future phase only)
